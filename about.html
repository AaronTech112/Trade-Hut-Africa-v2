Global Initiative

The Global Financial Clarity Initiative

Tradehut is the convener of the Global Financial Clarity Initiative (GFCI) — a global campaign focused on exposing fraudulent investment schemes, combatting cyber-enabled financial crime, and educating individuals to make informed, confident decisions.

GFCI Objectives

  • Educate 1 million Africans on responsible trading & investment
  • Promote safe financial practices to reduce online scams
  • Provide free clarity sessions, webinars, and investment guides
  • Encourage long-term wealth mentality over shortcuts

Current GFCI Programs

Africa Market Clarity Tour (20+ cities)
Campus Finance Awareness Drive
Women Building Wealth Program
Monthly Financial Stability Bootcamps

Why GFCI Matters

Financial misinformation is high in Africa. Scams thrive. GFCI is Tradehut's response — a structured, transparent movement helping people make safer, smarter financial decisions.
